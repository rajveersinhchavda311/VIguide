What is FFCS?

FFCS (Fully Flexible Credit System) is the course registration system used in our university. It allows students to choose their own courses, faculty, and timings based on availability and their academic requirements.

Key Features of FFCS:

  • Flexibility to choose courses across departments
  • Option to select preferred faculty members
  • Ability to create personalized timetables
  • Multiple rounds for course selection
  • Real-time seat availability updates

Strategic FFCS Planning

FFCS Survival Guide

  1. Mobile Access: Use "FFCS on the Go" for real-time slot tracking
  2. Peer Network:
    • Join student groups for strategy discussions
    • Consult seniors about professor styles
  3. Professor Research:
    • Check faculty reviews on forums
    • Balance between engaging vs. lenient professors

Timetable Priorities

  • Choose between morning vs afternoon productivity peaks
  • Maintain balance between:
    • Academic efficiency
    • Club activities
    • Free slots for self-study

Wishlist Warning

Always fill course wishlist to:

  • Help HOD gauge course demand
  • Prevent seat shortages
  • Avoid credit completion delays
Missing wishlist = Risk of unavailable preferred courses!

Important Notes:

The FFCS portal is typically open for limited periods during the semester. Make sure to complete your registration within the specified dates to avoid late registration penalties.
