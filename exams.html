Examination Information

The examination system at our university is designed to assess your understanding and knowledge of course material. Here's everything you need to know about exams, schedules, and preparation.

Exam Regulations:

All students must carry their university ID to examination halls. Electronic devices are strictly prohibited. Any form of malpractice will result in disciplinary action.

VIT Exam Structure

Continuous Assessment System

  • CAT 1 (15%): Limited syllabus, ideal for strong start
  • CAT 2 (15%): Open-book exam - bring recommended books
  • FAT (40%): Comprehensive final exam - requires early preparation
  • Quizzes (10%): Two per subject - can be online/offline
  • Digital Assignments (10%): Easy marks via timely submission

Pro Tips

  • CAT 2 Strategy:
    • Rent books from Balaji Books or main road sellers
    • Library books available 15 days in advance
    • Amazon hack: Buy & return after exams
  • PYQs Are Key:
    • Access previous papers through VIT Paper Vault
    • CodeChef VIT Papers repository
    • Exam patterns often repeat annually

Attendance Policy

  • 75% mandatory attendance for FAT eligibility
  • 9-pointer students exempt from attendance rules
  • Track attendance using VITian App
