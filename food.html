Best Restaurants Near Campus

Discover great dining options just minutes from campus. Here are our top picks for student-friendly restaurants with quality food at reasonable prices.

Tarama Hotel

₹500 for two

North Indian • Vegetarian

Authentic North Indian flavors with a focus on Punjabi cuisine. Famous for their butter chicken and garlic naan. The restaurant has a cozy ambiance perfect for family dinners and small gatherings. Open from 11AM to 11PM daily.

11AM - 11PM 1.2km from campus Veg & Non-Veg

McDonald's

₹400 for two

Fast Food • American

Global fast food chain serving burgers, fries, and shakes. Popular for quick meals and student discounts. Open 24/7 with WiFi available. Known for their McAloo Tikki burger and McFlurry desserts.

24 Hours 0.8km from campus Free WiFi

Burger King

₹450 for two

Fast Food • Burgers

Home of the Whopper. Flame-grilled burgers with student meal combos available. Special student discount of 15% on presentation of college ID. Try their Chicken Whopper and onion rings.

10AM - 12AM 1km from campus Student Discount

Subway

₹350 for two

Sandwiches • Healthy

Build-your-own sandwiches with fresh ingredients. Popular for customizable, healthier fast food options. Offers student meal deals and veggie delight options. Their cookies are a must-try!

8AM - 11PM 0.5km from campus Veg Options

Wow Momo

₹300 for two

Tibetan • Street Food

Specializing in momos with various fillings and sauces. Budget-friendly and quick service. Offers steamed, fried and pan-fried momos with exotic sauces. Their cheese momos are particularly popular.

11AM - 10PM 0.3km from campus Quick Service

The Hive

Varies by stall

Multi-Cuisine • Food Court

Central hub with multiple food stalls offering diverse options from Indian to Continental. Features live music on weekends and ample seating. Popular among student groups for its variety and lively atmosphere.

10AM - 11PM On-campus Live Music

Pizza Hut

₹600 for two

Pizza • Italian

Famous pizza chain with dine-in and delivery options. Student lunch specials available. Their unlimited pizza buffet (weekday lunches) is particularly popular among students. Don't miss their garlic breadsticks.

11AM - 11PM 1.5km from campus Lunch Buffet

Kailash Parvat

₹450 for two

Gujarati • Rajasthani

Authentic vegetarian thalis with unlimited servings. Great for traditional Indian meals. Known for their dal baati churma and kachoris. The restaurant has traditional decor with floor seating options available.

10AM - 10:30PM 2km from campus Pure Veg

Barbeque Nation

₹1200 for two

BBQ • Grill

Live grill on your table with unlimited starters. Special student discounts on weekdays. Their buffet includes a wide variety of Indian and international dishes. Perfect for celebrations and group outings.

12PM - 11PM 3km from campus Live Grill

Pastel Poetry

₹500 for two

Cafe • Continental

Instagram-worthy cafe with artisanal coffees and desserts. Great study spot with WiFi. Known for their rainbow cakes and specialty coffees. The pastel-themed decor makes it popular for photos.

8AM - 10PM 1.8km from campus Photogenic

Shills

₹400 for two

Bakery • Cafe

Cozy bakery known for croissants and sandwiches. Open late for night owls. Their midnight brownies and coffee combos are popular during exam seasons. Also offers healthy salad options.

7AM - 1AM 0.7km from campus Late Night

Belgian Waffle

₹300 for two

Desserts • Cafe

Specializing in authentic Belgian waffles with various toppings. Perfect for sweet cravings. Offers both classic and innovative flavors like red velvet and matcha. Their hot chocolate pairs perfectly with waffles.

9AM - 11PM 1.1km from campus Dessert Bar
